AISTATS-97 Registration Instructions

Instructions for Registering for the Workshop:
  1. View the latest information on the technical program and tutorial program .
  2. Fill out a copy of the registration form and mail with registration fees by December 2nd to get the benefit of early registration discounts.
  3. Make reservations at the conference hotel, note that December 20th is the cutoff date for which rooms will be held for the workshop.
